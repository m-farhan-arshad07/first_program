Facet wrap:¶

- Makes a long ribbon of panels (generated by any number of variables) and
- wraps it into 2d. This is useful in case of a single variable.

Facet grid:¶

- Make a matrix of panels using row and column faceting variables.
- It is most useful in case of two discrete variables.
